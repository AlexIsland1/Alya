Your first TIME visit in-clinic

The First Time Visit is prior to your first facial treatment. This is a 45-minute appointment in which you will leave with a full analysis of your unique skin conditions and gain an understanding of underlying issues at play through the use of Advanced Skin Analysis Technology.

Your First Time Visit is fully redeemable on Ragan Skin products during your first visit in-clinic. You’ll walk away knowing the health and condition of your skin, preventative actions to be taken, and appropriate products and supplements to benefit the overall health of your skin for long-term and sustainable results.

You will then proceed with your facial of choice from the treatment menu below.
